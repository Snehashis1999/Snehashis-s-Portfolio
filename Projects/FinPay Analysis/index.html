Project Overview

FinPay Analytics is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed to simulate the analytics workflow of a modern digital payments company.

Starting from a real-world fintech transaction dataset, the project demonstrates the complete BI lifecycle including data engineering, feature engineering, SQL analytics, dimensional modelling, interactive dashboards and executive reporting.

The primary objective is to convert raw payment transactions into meaningful business insights that support customer analytics, merchant performance monitoring and fraud detection.

Business Problem

Revenue Monitoring

Understand revenue generation across transaction types, customer segments and merchant categories.

Fraud Detection

Identify fraudulent transactions, estimate fraud exposure and evaluate detection effectiveness.

Customer Behaviour

Segment customers based on demographics, income and spending behaviour.

Merchant Performance

Compare merchant categories, settlement cycles and regional performance.

Key Business Insights

The analysis uncovered several actionable insights across executive reporting, customer behaviour, merchant performance and fraud detection.

📈 Executive

  • Very Large transactions contribute nearly 60% of total platform activity.
  • Revenue generation is concentrated across a few merchant states.
  • PAYMENT transactions contain the highest number of incomplete balance records.

👥 Customer

  • Standard customers generate the largest share of platform revenue.
  • Middle-income customers form more than half of the customer base.
  • Customers aged 26–35 years represent the largest user segment.

🏪 Merchant

  • Retail merchants generate the highest overall revenue.
  • Enterprise merchants outperform Small and Medium businesses.
  • Nearly 70% of merchants settle payments through T+1 cycles.

🛡 Fraud

  • TRANSFER transactions contribute the largest fraud exposure.
  • Retail and E-Commerce merchants account for most fraud losses.
  • The current rule-based detection system flags only a tiny fraction of fraud cases.

Business Recommendations

🤖

AI Fraud Detection

Introduce behavioural machine learning models to improve fraud detection accuracy beyond rule-based systems.

🔒

High Value Monitoring

Apply additional verification and risk scoring for large transfer transactions.

📊

Merchant Risk Scoring

Develop dynamic merchant risk profiles using fraud history and transaction behaviour.

⚙️

Data Quality

Improve balance reconciliation to reduce incomplete transaction records.
